The Hardware Design Support Group(GAPH) has as general objective the research and development of methods and tools for the design, implementation and validation of systems-on-chip (SoCs). Much of our current research addresses networks on chip (NoCs), multiprocessor systems-on-chip (MPSoCs) and non-synchronous circuits (either GALS and fully asynchronous). Do not miss ATLAS, our open source environment for NoC generation, traffic generation for NoCs, NoC simulation and NoC performance evaluation. Also, do not miss our experimental, open source synthesizable Multiprocessor System on a Chip HeMPS and ASCEnD, a library of standard-cells to implement asynchronous ASICs.
